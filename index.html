Top Five Places to Eat in Athens, Ohio

After spending four years in Athens I have finally found my favorite places to eat.

#5 JACKIE O'S BREWERY

Locally brewed we have Jackie O’s coming in at number 5. This restaurant has everything from crafted beer to their own local confections. My personal favorite is the pesto melt. This melt is savory and sweet. The perfect combination between feta and mozzarella cheese! How could you go wrong? They have a great selection of appetizers ranging from fried pickles to a charcuterie board, if you are a pizza lover like me you can’t go wrong with the BBQ chicken pizza!

#4 BRENNANS COFFEE AND CAFE

Being away from home can be hard at times, but Brennans helps make you feel at ease. Their food and warm coffee gives you that at home feeling that you have missed being away at School. Brennans has a wide selection of bagels, deli sandwiches, soups and salads. Most importantly they have coffee! They are known for their famous Chai Tea. My personal favorite a vanilla chai tea lattee. Their sandwiches are perfectly made with whole healthy ingredients.

#3 GINGER

As someone who just became a sushi lover ginger is the perfect spot for all your sushi needs. They also have fried rice and lo mein bowls that you can build yourself. The bowls have toppings such as carrots, cucumber, spicy chicken and sesame seeds. My personal favorite roll from Ginger is the spider and bobcat roll. The spider roll consists of crab, eel sauce, spinach and avocado, delicious!

#2 THE PIG SKIN

While the Pig Skin during the day is a hot place to eat on the town it is. also a popular place for those who are wanting a drink in the evening! This. Spot is known for their famous smoked wings and juicy burgers. With a variety of different sandwiches to select from they make it easy that you are going to enjoy your meal.

#1 BAGEL STREET DELI

Coming in at the top place to eat is the one and only Bagel Street Deli. You truly can’t go wrong with. Anything on their menu. It is all absolutely delicious., they have everything from breakfast sandwiches to your savory meat lovers duo. My personal favorite is the turkey tom, this includes turkey, lettuce, tomato, mayo and your choice of cheese!
